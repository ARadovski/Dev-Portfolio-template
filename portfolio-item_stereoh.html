Virtual concerts in 3D venues

Conceptualized and led production on a platform for virtual concerts based on Mozilla Hubs Cloud, designed and built 3D venues and sets for the events in Blender, maintained AWS server infrastructure, set up live streaming via OBS Studio, 2020 - pres.

The challenge was to produce a marketing in-person experience for app launch party at a major clothing retailer flagship location. The experince had to be engaging and easily accessible to customers, and representative of the brand's aesthetics and values.

As a part of small team at neoneon I rose to the challenge by conceptualizing an augmented reality treasure hunt game that was utilizing then-brand-new 8th Wall Web AR platform for ease of access (no app download required) and compatibility with a wide range of smart devices. I handled processing 3D assets and animation in Blender, including getting scanned by LensCloud photogrammetry array, as well as wrote AFrame code for 3D model behavior, JavaScript for scorekeeping, and used Howler.js library for dynamic soundtrack. I also designed prototypes of collectable physical markers for triggering the AR experience.
